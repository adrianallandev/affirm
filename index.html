The same power that raised Christ from the dead lives inside Adrian. God is for me, and that settles it.
"No weapon formed against you shall prosper… This is the heritage of the servants of the Lord."— Isaiah 54:17

✦ Adrian's Complete Declaration — Read This Aloud

I am Adrian — a child of God. Redeemed, loved, and chosen by the Creator of the universe. My faith is the foundation of everything. Today I choose to trust God completely, and I declare that no non-God force — no enemy, no darkness, no attack — will prevail against me or my family.


My will is iron. I face every test with unbreakable courage. No test will defeat me. I am refined by fire and I come out stronger every time.


My body is a temple — strong, sharp, and full of energy. I show up looking and feeling like the man I was made to be.


I am the smartest, sharpest person in any conversation I enter. My mind is trained, my words are powerful, and God has anointed my mouth. No quiz, no interview, no test will catch me off guard.


My joy is deep and it cannot be stolen. I choose gratitude. I choose peace. I walk in happiness that is my inheritance as a son of God.


I can speak to any leader, any executive, any powerful person — and I hold my own. I am credible, confident, and I add real value everywhere I go.


I act today. I do not procrastinate. I show up every day and move my life forward because God's purpose for Adrian is too important to delay.


I will find a great job. I will be financially set. God prospers the work of my hands.


I am grateful. I am grounded in Christ. I am ready for this day. Adrian wins — because God is with him.
